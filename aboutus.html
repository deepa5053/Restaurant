About Us Zoe’s Coffee owner, Zoe Young, settled in Newtown with a passion
to share the best elements of coffee - handcrafted espresso drinks and
high quality food items, such as fresh croissants, and delicious cookies Zoe has created a space where you can enjoy an hour or an afternoon
in a relaxed environment. Whether you look for some
one-on-one time with your friend or alone time with your book,
or if you need to get some work done, you are welcome at Zoe’s Coffee.
